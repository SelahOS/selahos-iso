A LETTER FROM THE FOUNDER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SelahOS began with a simple frustration:
why does creative software make us feel like
we're fighting the machine instead of making music?

I spent years watching talented people give up —
not because they lacked skill,
but because the tools got in the way.

SelahOS is my answer.

An operating system that gets out of your way.
That speaks the language of music, art, and creation.
That treats your old hardware like a sacred instrument,
not a reason to buy something new.

The name "Selah" comes from the Hebrew Psalms.
Scholars debate its exact meaning,
but most agree: it means to pause. To breathe.
To consider what you've just heard.

That's what I want your computer to let you do.

Pause. Reflect. Create.

— Dane-Brandon Noble
  Founder, Selah Technologies LLC
  2026

"Exodus 31:3 — And I have filled him with the Spirit of God,
 with wisdom, with understanding, with knowledge
 and with all kinds of skills."
