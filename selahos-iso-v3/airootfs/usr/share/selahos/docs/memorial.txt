TWA FLIGHT 800 — IN MEMORIAM

July 17, 1996.

TWA Flight 800 departed New York's John F. Kennedy International Airport
at 8:19 PM. Twelve minutes later, at 8:31 PM, the Boeing 747-100
exploded over the Atlantic Ocean near East Moriches, New York.

All 230 souls aboard were lost.

We remember them.

This software is dedicated to their memory,
and to every life cut short before its time.

"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted
 and saves those who are crushed in spirit."
